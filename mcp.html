Real prompts, real answers

What people actually ask.

Every tier supports natural language. Click a question to see the underlying tools, the data returned, and what your AI does with it.

"Audit my wasted spend last week."
Agency
Claude calls get_campaigns and get_search_terms, filters search terms with $20+ in spend and zero conversions, and returns a ranked list with negative-keyword recommendations.
Sample output $487 wasted on 12 search terms · top offender: "kettle replacement" ($94, 0 conv) · 8 negatives suggested
"Which SKUs are about to stock out?"
Solo seller
get_inventory returns days-of-cover by SKU and FC. Claude flags anything under 14 days and groups by reorder priority.
Sample output 3 SKUs critical: B09XYZ (6 days), B08AAA (12 days), B05DDD (13 days) · suggested PO sizes attached
"Compare June to May for all profiles."
Brand manager
get_sales and get_campaigns with two date ranges. Claude builds a side-by-side delta and highlights statistically significant moves.
Sample output Revenue +14% MoM · ad spend +6% · ROAS +7% · 2 profiles dragging average down
"Find negative keywords for B09XYZ."
Solo seller
get_search_terms filtered to one ASIN; Claude scores each by ACoS contribution and proposes 10–20 negatives ranked by impact.
Sample output 18 candidates · top 5 negatives would save ~$220/wk at current pace
"How am I trending vs. last quarter?"
Agency
get_sales with rolling 90-day windows. Claude computes WoW and MoM rate-of-change and surfaces inflection points.
Sample output Q2 revenue +22% vs Q1 · acceleration in week 8 · driven by Halo Kettle Prime promo
"Which keywords have I never bid on but should?"
Brand manager · PLUS
get_brand_analytics + get_keywords. Claude identifies high-volume search terms with low-share and no active campaign coverage.
Sample output 7 keywords with 5K+ monthly searches and {"<"}10% impression share · ready to draft a campaign
