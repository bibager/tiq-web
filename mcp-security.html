MCP Security & Privacy

Your data stays yours.

We take security seriously. Here's how we protect your Amazon data, your credentials, and your privacy — at every layer.

OAuth 2.0 — No password access

We never see your Amazon password. You authorize via Amazon's official OAuth flow — Amazon issues a scoped token directly to TrackIQ.

  • Token auto-renews for 12 months
  • Revoke access anytime from Seller Central
  • We cannot modify your listings or account settings

Data encryption in transit and at rest

All communication between you, TrackIQ, and Amazon is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256.

  • TLS 1.3 for all API calls
  • AES-256 for stored data
  • Keys managed via AWS KMS

No training on your data

Your Amazon data is never used to train AI models. We don't share it, sell it, or feed it into anyone's LLM.

  • Your data is yours — period
  • No cross-customer aggregation
  • Delete anytime from account settings

Short-lived caching

We cache API responses for performance and rate-limiting. Caches expire within 24 hours.

  • 24-hour max cache lifetime
  • Cached data encrypted at rest
  • Cache cleared on token revocation

Write actions require confirmation

All write actions (bid changes, budget moves, negative keywords) require explicit confirmation in your AI assistant before execution.

  • No auto-execution of write tools
  • Audit log maintained for 24 months
  • Rollback support for most actions

Role-based access control

For agencies: assign read-only or full-access roles per user. Admins can revoke access instantly.

  • Per-user permission levels
  • Activity logs per account
  • Instant revocation

Security FAQ

What data does TrackIQ access?

Only what you authorize via Amazon's OAuth flow. Read tools cover sales, orders, returns, ad performance, inventory, and (on Plus tier) Brand Analytics and AMC audiences. Write tools (Plus only) can modify keyword bids, budgets, and negative keywords — each requiring explicit confirmation.

Can TrackIQ change my product listings or prices?

No. Our OAuth scopes do not include listing or pricing APIs. We can read sales and ad data, and (on Plus tier) adjust ad bids and budgets — but we cannot touch your catalog, pricing, or account settings.

How long do you store my data?

We cache API responses for up to 24 hours for performance. Historical data (for lookback queries) is stored per your tier: 30 days on Free, 12 months on Lite, 24 months on Plus. You can delete all data anytime from Account Settings.

Do you train AI models on my Amazon data?

No. Your data is used only to answer your queries via your AI assistant. We don't aggregate, share, or train models on it.

What happens if I revoke access?

Tools stop working immediately. Cached data is purged within 24 hours. Historical data remains until you explicitly delete it from Account Settings.

Is TrackIQ PCI compliant?

We don't handle payment card data. Billing is via Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1). We never see your card number.

Can I audit what TrackIQ accessed?

Yes. Every tool call is logged with timestamp, user, and tool name. Logs are available in Account Settings → Activity for 90 days (Free/Lite) or 24 months (Plus).

Where is my data stored?

AWS us-east-1 (Virginia). Data does not leave the US. EU data residency available on request for Enterprise customers.

How do I report a security issue?

Email security@trackiq.com. We respond to security reports within 24 hours and maintain a public security hall of fame.
