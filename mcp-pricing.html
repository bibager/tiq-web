FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a credit card for Free?

No. The Free tier requires only an Amazon Seller Central account. You'll never see a payment screen unless you choose to upgrade.

Can I use my own Claude or ChatGPT subscription?

Yes — that's the whole point. TrackIQ MCP is an MCP server. You connect it to whichever AI assistant you already pay for. We don't charge for AI tokens; we charge for data access.

What's the difference between MCP and the platform?

The TrackIQ Platform is a full web app with dashboards, automated reports, and Aria, our AI agent. The MCP is lighter — it gives your AI assistant read (and optionally write) access to your Amazon data through standardized tools. Different surfaces, different price points.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Paid tiers are month-to-month. Cancel from account settings; access continues through the end of the billing period.

What counts as a "tool call"?

A single invocation by your AI assistant — for example, asking "how were my sales yesterday?" might use one or two tool calls. A typical conversation runs 3–8 calls. Free's 50/day cap covers most casual exploration.

What data does TrackIQ access?

Only what you authorize via Amazon's official OAuth flow. Read tools cover sales, ads, and inventory. Write tools (Plus only) require explicit confirmation in your AI assistant before each action. Full details on the Security page.

Does TrackIQ store my Amazon data?

We cache responses for performance and rate-limiting. Caches expire within 24 hours. We don't train models on your data. You can revoke access and trigger deletion at any time.

How many AI assistants can I connect?

One TrackIQ account, any number of MCP-compatible clients. Use Claude Desktop on your laptop, Claude.ai in the browser, and Cursor for coding — same data, same limits.

Can agencies manage multiple seller accounts?

Today, each TrackIQ account maps to one Amazon profile. For multi-brand agency setups, the TrackIQ Platform is the right product — agency white-label MCP is on the v2 roadmap.

What happens if I hit my daily limit?

Tools return a clear "limit reached" message; your AI assistant tells you so you can upgrade or wait until the daily reset. No surprise charges, no overage billing.
