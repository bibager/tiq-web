The four pillars

What Aria actually does.

Four analyst-grade jobs running continuously — no prompts, no new dashboards, no extra headcount. Every pillar is available from day one, on every plan.

01
Root Cause Analysis

Instant answers to “why?”

Why did revenue drop Tuesday? The agent investigates across ads, inventory, Buy Box, pricing, and seasonality — then delivers a ranked explanation.

  • Cross-signal diagnosis, ranked by contribution
  • Plain-English explanations, not chart dumps
  • One-click follow-up questions
Example: “Revenue dipped 16% WoW. 58% explained by Buy Box loss on B09XYZ, 27% by SB campaign overspend, 15% by suppressed listing B08AAA.”
02
Agentic Ad Optimization · IRO

Incremental Revenue Optimization

Our patent-pending IRO engine reallocates spend to the SKUs that drive new revenue — not vanity ROAS. Proposes moves, quantifies lift, waits for your approval.

  • Patent 63/985,607 · Economic Impact-Based Confidence
  • Model-backed counterfactuals, not rules-of-thumb
  • Approval workflow — agent never spends without a human
Example: “Shift $8.4K from branded search to SD on 6 hero SKUs. Projected +$127K incremental revenue this month at 94% confidence.”
03
Intelligent Alerting

Catch issues in hours, not days

Listing suppressions, Buy Box loss, overspend, stockouts — routed to the right person with severity scores and suggested fixes attached.

  • Per-role subscriptions (buyer, PPC lead, director)
  • Severity scoring & automatic deduplication
  • Fix suggestions inline — not just “something broke”
Example: “B09XYZ lost Buy Box to Seller X (price $24.99 vs yours $27.49). Suggested: match to $25.49, projected Buy Box recovery 78%.”
04
Automated Reporting

Reports delivered, not demanded

WBRs, client updates, and exec digests — generated, formatted, and posted. Nobody chases a slide deck on a Sunday night.

  • Monday WBRs auto-posted to Slack
  • Per-client reports for agencies · white-label ready
  • Custom KPI sets, scheduled or on-demand
Example: “Monday 8:00 AM: WBR posted to #kettleco-ops. Sales $308K (−16.8% WoW), 2 anomalies flagged, 1 replenishment needed.”
